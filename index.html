My Story

How I Got Here

Started in 2020 and never really stopped.

2020 — The Start
Opened Roblox Studio for the First Time
Started playing around with Roblox Studio with no idea what I was doing. Wrote my first scripts, broke a lot of things, and slowly figured out how Lua actually worked.
2021–2022 — Finding My Thing
Discovered Animations Were My Strength
Spent a lot of time in the Animation Editor and realized this was what I was good at. Started making animations for other people and getting paid in Robux for it.
2023–2024 — Building More
Combining Animations and Scripting
Started combining my animation work with scripting — hooking everything up properly, building systems around it, and making full game features that actually felt good to play.
2025 — Shipped
Finished and Released My First Game
Finally put out a complete game. Built everything myself — the scripts, the animations, the whole thing. Probably the most I've learned in a single project.

One Thing I Want You to Know

I Keep It Real

Everything I make is done by hand. No AI-generated scripts, no stolen assets, no shortcuts. When you work with me, every animation was made by me and every line of code was written by me. I've put in the time since 2020 to actually get good at this — and that's exactly what you're getting.
